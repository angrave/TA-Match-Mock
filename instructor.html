Instructor TA Request Form

Fill out one form per class you are teaching in the coming year.

Please choose a faculty member from the Acting as dropdown in the header to begin.
Instructions: Fill out one form for every class you are teaching in the coming year. Answer the questions below, aiming to be as inclusive as possible about absolute requirements as well as preferences. Please realize that most MS/MSc students and PhD students past year 5 are not guaranteed support (some MS/MSc students do have a funding guarantee — those are flagged in the lists below); therefore, if your requirements for TAship eliminate all students who are guaranteed support, it is possible you will not be assigned a TA.
Recommendations: This questionnaire asks you to list students you prefer and students you do not wish to have as TAs. Any student you do not list as either preferred or specifically not wanted will be considered acceptable. We strongly recommend that you speak with students you list as preferred in advance, so they know to also select your course as preferred.
